Shipping & Returns

Last Updated: October 30, 2024

At Lovable Supply, we want you to love your merchandise. This page contains important information about our shipping and returns policies.

1. Shipping Information

1.1 Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays). You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been shipped with tracking information.

1.2 Shipping Methods & Times

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Cost
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days $5.99
Express Shipping 2-3 business days $12.99
Overnight Shipping 1 business day $24.99
Free Shipping 5-7 business days Free on orders over $75

Note: Shipping times are estimates and may vary based on your location and carrier delays. International shipping times may be longer.

1.3 International Shipping

We currently ship to the following regions:

  • United States (all 50 states)
  • Canada
  • United Kingdom
  • European Union countries
  • Australia and New Zealand

International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the customer. Delivery times vary by country (typically 7-21 business days).

1.4 Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on the carrier's website.

2. Returns Policy

2.1 Return Eligibility

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items. To be eligible for a return, items must be:

  • Unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Not personalized or custom-made items
  • Not final sale items (marked as such at checkout)

2.2 How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact our customer service team at merch@lovable.dev within 30 days of receiving your order
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. We will provide you with a return authorization and shipping instructions
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  5. Ship the item to the provided return address

Important: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or we made an error with your order.

2.3 Refund Processing

Once we receive your return, we will inspect the item and process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. Please note:

  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable (except for defective items or our errors)
  • It may take 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account
  • You will receive an email confirmation once your refund has been processed

3. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size or color, please return the original item and place a new order for the desired item.

4. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at merch@lovable.dev with:

  • Your order number
  • Photos of the damaged or defective item
  • Description of the issue

We will provide a prepaid return label and send you a replacement item or issue a full refund, including original shipping costs.

5. Wrong Item Received

If you receive the wrong item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will provide a prepaid return label and ship you the correct item at no additional cost.

6. Lost or Stolen Packages

We are not responsible for packages that are lost or stolen after delivery confirmation. If your tracking shows the package was delivered but you did not receive it:

  • Check with neighbors or building management
  • Verify the shipping address was correct
  • Contact the carrier to file a claim
  • Contact us for assistance

7. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable products
  • Personal items (for hygiene reasons)
  • Items marked as final sale
  • Items returned without prior authorization

8. Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of purchase. After this time, orders enter processing and cannot be cancelled. Please contact us immediately if you need to cancel an order.

9. Questions?

If you have any questions about our shipping or returns policy, please don't hesitate to contact us:

Email: merch@lovable.dev
Twitter: @lovable_dev

We're here to help and want to ensure you have a great experience with Lovable Supply!
