Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Lovable Supply

Orders & Payment

How do I place an order? +

Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our products, click "Add to Cart" on items you want, then click the cart icon to review your order and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to enter your shipping and payment information.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Can I modify or cancel my order? +

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 2 hours of purchase. After that, orders enter processing and cannot be changed. Please contact us immediately at merch@lovable.dev if you need to make changes.

Do you offer gift cards? +

Yes! Digital gift cards are available in various denominations. They're perfect for letting fellow developers choose their favorite merch. Gift cards never expire and can be used for any purchase on our site.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take? +

Shipping times depend on your selected method:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: 1 business day

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days before shipping. See our Shipping & Returns page for more details.

Do you ship internationally? +

Yes! We ship to the US, Canada, UK, EU countries, Australia, and New Zealand. International shipping typically takes 7-21 business days. Customs duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

How can I track my order? +

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on the carrier's website. If you haven't received tracking information within 3 business days, please contact us.

Is free shipping available? +

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $75 within the United States. International orders and expedited shipping have additional fees.

Products

What sizes are available? +

Our apparel is available in sizes XS through 3XL. Each product page includes a detailed size chart to help you find the perfect fit. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

What are the products made of? +

We use high-quality, sustainable materials:

  • T-shirts: 100% organic cotton or cotton/poly blend
  • Hoodies: 80% cotton, 20% recycled polyester
  • Stickers: Durable vinyl with weather-resistant coating

All materials are ethically sourced and produced.

How do I care for my merchandise? +

To keep your merch looking great:

  • Machine wash cold with like colors
  • Tumble dry low or hang dry
  • Do not bleach or dry clean
  • Turn inside out before washing
  • Iron on low heat if needed (avoid designs)
Will there be restocks of sold-out items? +

We do our best to restock popular items, but some designs are limited edition. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified about restocks and new product launches!

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy? +

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unworn, unwashed items in original condition with tags attached. Contact us at merch@lovable.dev to initiate a return. See our Shipping & Returns page for complete details.

Do you offer exchanges? +

We don't offer direct exchanges at this time. If you need a different size or item, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the item you'd like.

What if my item is defective or damaged? +

We're sorry if you received a defective or damaged item! Contact us immediately with photos of the issue, and we'll send you a replacement or provide a full refund, including original shipping costs. We'll provide a prepaid return label.

Account & Support

Do I need an account to place an order? +

No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, save your shipping information, and receive exclusive updates about new products and promotions.

How do I reset my password? +

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

How can I contact customer support? +

You can reach us at:

  • Email: merch@lovable.dev
  • Twitter: @lovable_dev

We typically respond within 24 hours during business days.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help! Get in touch with us and we'll respond as soon as possible.
