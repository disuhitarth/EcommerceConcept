Store Policies

Last Updated: October 30, 2024

Welcome to Lovable Supply! These store policies outline important information about shopping with us, including our commitments to quality, customer service, and ethical business practices.

1. Our Customer Commitment

At Lovable Supply, we're committed to providing you with:

  • High-quality merchandise that meets our strict standards
  • Fair and transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Fast, reliable shipping and order processing
  • Responsive customer service and support
  • A hassle-free return process for eligible items
  • Protection of your personal information and privacy

2. Product Quality Standards

Materials & Manufacturing

All our products meet the following standards:

  • Made from premium, sustainable materials whenever possible
  • Manufactured in facilities that meet ethical labor standards
  • Undergo quality control inspections before shipping
  • Designed for durability and long-term use
  • Environmentally conscious production practices

Quality Guarantee: If you're not satisfied with the quality of your purchase, contact us within 30 days and we'll make it right.

3. Pricing & Payment

Pricing Policy

All prices are listed in USD and include:

  • Base product cost
  • Any applicable product customization

Prices do NOT include:

  • Shipping costs (calculated at checkout)
  • Customs duties or import taxes for international orders
  • State or local sales tax where applicable

Payment Methods

We accept the following secure payment methods:

  • All major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay

All transactions are processed securely and we never store your complete payment information.

4. Order Processing

Order Confirmation

You will receive an email confirmation immediately after placing your order. This confirms we've received your order but does not mean it has shipped yet.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days. During peak seasons or promotions, processing may take slightly longer.

Order Changes & Cancellations

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of purchase. After this time, orders enter our fulfillment process and cannot be changed. Contact us immediately at merch@lovable.dev if you need to make changes.

5. Shipping Policy

For detailed shipping information, please see our Shipping & Returns page. Key points:

  • Free standard shipping on orders over $75 (US only)
  • Multiple shipping speed options available
  • International shipping to select countries
  • Tracking provided for all orders
  • We're not responsible for carrier delays beyond our control

6. Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase! Our return policy includes:

  • 30-day return window for most items
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • Full refund to original payment method
  • Prepaid return labels for defective items
  • Customer pays return shipping for change-of-mind returns

See our full Shipping & Returns policy for complete details.

7. Customer Service

How to Reach Us

Email: merch@lovable.dev
Twitter: @lovable_dev

Response Times

We typically respond to inquiries within 4-6 hours during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST). Weekend inquiries are answered on the next business day.

8. Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously:

  • We never sell your personal information
  • All transactions are encrypted and secure
  • We only collect information necessary to process orders
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time

Read our complete Privacy Policy for full details.

9. Intellectual Property

All designs, logos, and content on Lovable Supply are protected by copyright and trademark laws. Unauthorized use or reproduction is prohibited.

10. Sustainability Commitment

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact:

  • Sustainable and ethically-sourced materials
  • Minimal, recyclable packaging
  • Carbon-neutral shipping options available
  • Support for environmental initiatives
  • Continuous improvement of our sustainability practices

11. Promotions & Discounts

Promotional codes and discounts:

  • Cannot be combined with other offers unless stated
  • Must be applied at checkout (cannot be added after purchase)
  • May have expiration dates and restrictions
  • We reserve the right to modify or cancel promotions at any time

12. Product Availability

While we strive to keep all products in stock:

  • Some items may be limited edition or seasonal
  • Stock levels are updated regularly but not guaranteed
  • We reserve the right to limit quantities per customer
  • Out-of-stock items may be cancelled and fully refunded

13. Policy Updates

We may update these policies from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our store constitutes acceptance of updated policies.

Questions about our policies? Contact us at merch@lovable.dev and we'll be happy to help!
