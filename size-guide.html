How to Measure

For the most accurate fit, measure yourself or a similar garment you already own:

📏 Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure horizontal and close under the arms.

📐 Length

Measure from the highest point of the shoulder down to the hem of the garment.

✋ Sleeve

For tees, measure from the shoulder seam to the sleeve hem. For hoodies, measure from the center back neck to the cuff.

💡 Sizing Tip: If you're between sizes or prefer a looser fit, we recommend going up one size. Our garments are pre-shrunk, but we still recommend following care instructions to maintain the best fit.
